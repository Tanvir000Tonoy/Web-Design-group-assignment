About FITNIX

FITNIX is a women-only fitness club built around safety, confidence, and sustainable progress. We focus on smart training, supportive coaching, and a welcoming environment for all fitness levels.

Our Purpose

What drives FITNIX — and how we support every member’s journey.

Mission

To provide a safe and motivating fitness space where women can train with confidence, build healthy habits, and achieve personal goals without judgment.

Vision

To become a trusted women’s fitness community that inspires long-term wellness through education, discipline, and positive support.

Values

  • Respect: Every body is welcome.
  • Safety: A comfortable women-only environment.
  • Progress: Realistic and measurable improvement.
  • Community: Support over comparison.

What We Offer

Programs designed for beginners, intermediates, and advanced members.

Personal Training

One-on-one coaching with structured programs focused on strength, mobility, and consistent results. Ideal for members who want guidance, accountability, and customised progression.

  • Goal setting and fitness assessment
  • Technique and form correction
  • Weekly plan with progress tracking

Group Classes

Fun and energising sessions that improve fitness while building confidence in a supportive setting. Great for motivation and routine.

  • HIIT & Conditioning
  • Strength Circuits
  • Yoga / Stretch & Mobility

Strength Training Zone

A dedicated space for strength-based workouts with equipment suitable for all levels. Trainers are available to support safe technique and progression.

  • Dumbbells & resistance tools
  • Beginner-friendly strength programs
  • Safe lifting guidance

Nutrition & Lifestyle Support

Fitness results are not only about training — we support lifestyle habits with basic guidance, routines, and educational resources.

  • Healthy habit checklists
  • Simple meal planning tips
  • Recovery and sleep guidance

Why Women Choose FITNIX

Designed to feel comfortable, structured, and genuinely supportive.

Women-Only Comfort

Beginner Friendly
